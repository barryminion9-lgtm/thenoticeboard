The Notice Board

Cape Town's free community noticeboard — connecting locals with events, services, markets, opportunities and everything in between.

About Us

The Notice Board is a free digital noticeboard for the Cape Town community. We believe in keeping things local, accessible and open. Whether you're hosting a yoga class in Sea Point, selling homemade koeksisters in Wynberg, or looking for a handyman in Plumstead — this is your board.

This platform is operated by a group of passionate designers and builders who want your life to benefit by what we create... we are a proudly Cape Town based company of thinkers, feelers and makers. Find us at thenoticeboard.co.za.
